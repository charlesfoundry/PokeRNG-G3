# Privacy Policy

PokeRNG G3 does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

The app stores user-entered RNG settings locally on the device, including game
version, TID, SID, default seed, saved targets, egg settings, and language
preference. This data is used only to restore app state and is not sent to any
server.

The app does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, tracking, accounts, or
third-party sign-in.

Sound and haptic feedback are used only for the in-app timer. No microphone,
camera, location, contacts, photos, or Bluetooth access is requested.

If you have questions, open an issue on the project repository:
https://github.com/charlesfoundry/PokeRNG-G3
